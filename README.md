# 📊 IBM HR Employee Attrition Analytics

### End-to-End HR Analytics Project | Excel • PostgreSQL • Power BI • DAX

---

## 📌 Project Overview

Employee attrition is one of the biggest workforce challenges organizations face. High turnover increases recruitment costs, lowers productivity, affects employee morale, and disrupts business continuity.

This project presents an end-to-end HR analytics solution using the **IBM HR Analytics Employee Attrition & Performance** dataset to identify the key factors influencing employee attrition and provide data-driven recommendations that can improve employee retention.

The project demonstrates the complete analytics lifecycle—from data preparation to executive dashboard reporting—using industry-standard analytics tools.

### Analytics Workflow

**Excel → PostgreSQL → SQL Analysis → Power BI → DAX → HR Business Recommendations**

# 🎯 Business Problem

The HR Director has observed increasing employee turnover and wants to understand:

- Why employees are leaving
- Which departments experience the highest attrition
- Which employee groups are at greater risk
- The major factors contributing to employee turnover
- What HR can do to improve employee retention

The objective of this project is to transform HR data into actionable insights that support workforce planning and evidence-based retention strategies.

---

# 🎯 Project Objectives

The analysis aims to:

- Measure the overall employee attrition rate.
- Identify departments with the highest employee turnover.
- Analyze job roles most affected by attrition.
- Evaluate the relationship between overtime and employee turnover.
- Determine whether salary influences retention.
- Investigate which age groups are more likely to leave.
- Assess the impact of work-life balance on attrition.
- Identify employees who meet predefined high-risk criteria.
- Provide actionable recommendations to improve employee retention.

---

# ❓ Business Questions

This project answers the following HR business questions:

1. What is the overall employee attrition rate?
2. Which departments lose the most employees?

5. Does salary affect employee retention?
6. Which age group experiences the highest attrition?
7. Does work-life balance influence attrition?
8. Which employees meet the highest-risk criteria?
9. What recommendations can HR implement to reduce employee attrition?
